## Hey there

I'm Jacopo — an IT Systems Engineer with a strong focus on security and a growing passion for AI. I've spent the last few years building and maintaining infrastructure for organizations of all sizes, from tightening cloud postures to wrangling Atlassian platforms at scale.

This blog is where I'll share what I learn along the way: practical tips, project write-ups, lessons from the field, and the occasional deep-dive into something that caught my attention.

## What to expect

- **Atlassian administration** — advanced Jira and Confluence workflows, automation, and governance.
- **Security practices** — hardening, incident response, and tooling for small-to-mid-size teams.
- **AI experiments** — how I use AI tools in my day-to-day engineering work.
- **Infrastructure notes** — cloud, networking, and everything in between.

If any of that sounds useful, stick around. More posts coming soon.
